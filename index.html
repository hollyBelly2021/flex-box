BIANCA'S TRAVEL GOALS

JAPAN

Japan is known for its unique and diverse culinary experiences, and maid cafes are a distinct part of its pop culture. Maid cafes originated in Akihabara, Tokyo, and have gained popularity for their quirky and entertaining atmosphere. In these cafes, waitresses dress in maid costumes and address customers as "masters" or "mistresses."

THAILAND

Thailand is renowned for its spicy cuisine, featuring a vibrant array of dishes that tantalize the taste buds. Tom Yum, a hot and sour soup with fragrant herbs, is a popular choice. Green Curry, a spicy coconut milk-based curry with aromatic herbs, is another spicy Thai delight.

SAGADA

Whang-Od is a legendary Filipina tattoo artist from the Kalinga tribe known for her traditional hand-tap tattooing technique. Using thorns and soot, she creates intricate designs reflecting cultural symbols and heritage. Many travelers seek her out in the village of Buscalan to receive a Whang-Od tattoo as a symbol of cultural appreciation and personal significance.

SWITZERLAND

Switzerland is renowned for its pristine snow-covered landscapes, especially in the winter months. The Swiss Alps offer world-class skiing and snowboarding opportunities, attracting winter sports enthusiasts from around the globe. The powdery snow in Switzerland is often light and fluffy, creating ideal conditions for winter activities.
